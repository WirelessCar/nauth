## Why NAuth?

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Kubernetes Native">
    <IconKubernetes slot="icon" class="icon" size="1.333em" />

  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Secure by Default">
    <FluentColorShieldCheckmark48 slot="icon" class="icon" size="1.333em" />
